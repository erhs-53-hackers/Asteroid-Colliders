Welcome to Asteroid Colliders

Asteroid Colliders was a project started by the programming team of the robotics club at Eleanor Roosevelt High School in Greenbelt, Maryland. The game was started as a simple project by one of the members of the team, and was later expanded to encompass and teach others basic OOP skills.

About the authors

The game was originally started by Nick (@nyaculak), and was co-programmed alongside Michael (@MichaelStevens).

Try it out!
